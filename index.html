Pork Adobo Recipe

Basic Filipino Pork Adobo with Soy Sauce, Vinegar, and Garlic. This delicious dish is perfect when served over newly cooked white rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons garlic minced or crushed
  • 5 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorn

Instructions:

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour.
    • Marinate overnight if possible.
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly then cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour remaining marinade including garlic.
  4. Add water, whole pepper corn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
    • You can add fresh chilies or red pepper flakes if you want it spicy.
    • Boil pork until tender and with that melt in your mouth goodness!
  5. Put-in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
    • Don't stir the pot after adding the vinegar.
    • Let the vinegar simmer for a few minutes before stirring.
  6. Recheck seasoning.
    • Add salt and/or pepper to taste.
